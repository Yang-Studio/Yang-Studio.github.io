Auk Odyssey

In the frozen future of 2042, a muscular penguin awakens to save the day!

Learn More

About the Game

Game Genre

Action RPG (ARPG)

Target Platforms

PC (possible future console releases)

Target Audience

Fans of sci-fi, biotechnology, humorous storytelling, and engaging action gameplay

Story & Characters

Protagonist – Pip

An adorably innocent penguin with an exaggerated muscular physique. Equipped with super strength ("One Punch" glass-breaking power) and comedic sliding abilities that let him crash through walls.

Antagonist – The Scientist

A scientist genetically modifying humans with penguin DNA for survival in extreme cold. A planned future boss fight will pit Pip against this mastermind of the laboratory.

Penguin NPCs

Fellow penguins awaiting rescue throughout the laboratory, who guide players to exits and hidden weapon locations.

Gameplay & Key Highlights

Exploration & Puzzle-solving

Discover humorous hidden items and easter eggs throughout the laboratory, especially the hidden hammer that unlocks new gameplay possibilities.

Combat Mechanics

Experience Pip's humorous combat animations and unique sliding mechanics that interact with the environment in unexpected and entertaining ways.

Player Zoo (Experimental Sandbox)

The main showcase area serves as a testing ground for player skills and interactions, featuring weapons, NPC interactions, and entertaining sliding mechanics.

Visual & Audio Design

Visual Style

Icy, futuristic, technological environments that create the perfect backdrop for Pip's adventures through the laboratory.

Sound Design

Humorous sound effects combined with intense, electronic background music that enhances the gameplay experience.

Future Development

The first chapter is complete, with boss fight and further chapters planned for future development.
